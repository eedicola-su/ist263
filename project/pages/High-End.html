Dior Cosmetics

Dior Cosmetics was created in 1969 under the Christian Dior name. Dior Cosmetics is sold at a variety of locations including their own stores, kiosks within larger department stores such as Macy's, Nordstroms, Dillard's, JCPenny, and Sephora stores. They are also available online at their website.
