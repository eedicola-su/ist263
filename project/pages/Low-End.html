Nyx Cosmetics

Nyx Cosmetics is a drugstore makeup brand priced inexpensively in order to give customers the ability to get quality makeup at a lower price point. Nyx is a subsidiary of L'Oreal Cosmetics which also owns makeup brands like Maybelline, IT Cosmetics, Urban Decay, and many others. Nyx is sold at a variety of retailers including Target, Ulta, and their own Nyx stores. In the video below, Jeffree reviews a full face of products from this brand to give an honest review of them.
